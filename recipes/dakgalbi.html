Dakgalbi Recipe

Home

Description

Dakgalbi is a Korean dish that is made with chicken and vegetables. It is a popular dish in Korea and is often served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces.
  2. Cut the onion and green bell pepper into small pieces.
  3. In a pot, add the chicken and water and bring to a boil.
  4. Add the onion and green bell pepper and cook until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Serve with rice and kimchi.
