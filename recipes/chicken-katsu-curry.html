Chicken Katsu Curry Recipe

Home

Description

Chicken Katsu Curry is a Japanese dish that is made with chicken, rice, and curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of curry sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken.
  2. Cook the rice.
  3. Cook the curry sauce.
  4. Serve the chicken katsu curry.
