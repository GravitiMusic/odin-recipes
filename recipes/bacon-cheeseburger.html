Bacon Cheeseburger Recipe

Home

Description

A bacon cheeseburger is a burger with bacon and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of bacon
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of bread

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon.
  2. Cook the ground beef.
  3. Add the cheese to the ground beef.
  4. Add the bread to the ground beef.
  5. Add the bacon to the ground beef.
  6. Serve the bacon cheeseburger.
